About Me

Hi, I'm Uday Tamang from Kathmandu, Nepal. My journey started in the kitchen as a chef, where I honed skills like teamwork, creativity, and understanding what people need. Over time, I realized I wanted to bring my passion for creating experiences into the digital world.
